Structure is Important

After working on Pspeak I was tasked with building out a brochure site for one of our clients CVET, a software company that acts a s a platform for quantifying the goods and services being offered by businesses. The site was in need of some serious structure, and needed to be built in a way that would allow for additional content to be added painlessly in the future.

In order to give the site some structure I decided to switch it over to the Sillex web framework. I chose Sillex becuse it was a PHP based micro-frame work made up of Symfony components so any one of the other engineers in the office could easily make upates if need be. I decided that becuse the site was only static content, and didn't require and ORM or any way to connect to a database that the full blown Symfony2 framework would require too much overhead.

In addition to the structural changes I added some updates to make the site more mobile responsive.
